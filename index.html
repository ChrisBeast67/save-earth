📚 Earth Facts

🌡️

Global Warming

Earth's temperature has risen 1.1°C since 1900. We need to keep it below 1.5°C!

🌊

Sea Level Rise

Sea levels are rising 3.4mm per year. Islands and coastal cities are at risk!

🦋

Species Extinction

We're losing 10,000 species per year. 1 million species are at risk of extinction!

🌳

Deforestation

27 football fields of forest are lost every minute. Trees are Earth's lungs!

🧊

Arctic Ice

Arctic sea ice is shrinking 13% per decade. Polar bears need their home!

💨

Air Pollution

90% of people breathe polluted air. Clean air is a basic right!

💡 How You Can Help

  • 💡 Save Energy - Turn off lights when leaving a room!
  • 🚿 Save Water - Take shorter showers!
  • 🛍️ Use Less Plastic - Bring reusable bags!
  • 🚲 Walk or Bike - Reduce car emissions!
  • 🌱 Plant Trees - Trees clean our air!
  • ♻️ Recycle - Sort your trash properly!
  • 📄 Save Paper - Use digital documents!
  • 🍎 Eat Local - Support local farmers!
