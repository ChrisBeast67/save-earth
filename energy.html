⚡ Renewable vs Non-Renewable Energy

What's the Difference?

Renewable energy comes from sources that don't run out - like sunlight, wind, and water. Non-renewable energy comes from fossil fuels that will eventually run out - like coal, oil, and natural gas.

☀️

Renewable Energy

Energy from sources that naturally replenish and won't run out.

⛽

Non-Renewable Energy

Energy from fossil fuels that take millions of years to form.

⚔️ Renewable vs Non-Renewable

✅ Renewable

  • Never runs out
  • Clean - no pollution
  • Low operating costs
  • Good for the planet
  • Available everywhere
  • Creates jobs

❌ Non-Renewable

  • Will run out someday
  • Causes pollution
  • Expensive to maintain
  • Bad for health
  • Creates climate change
  • Finite resources
